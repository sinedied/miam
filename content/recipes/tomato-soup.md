1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat and sauté the chopped onion until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
2. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
3. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and vegetable stock, then stir in the sugar and salt.
4. Bring to a simmer and cook uncovered for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
5. Blend the soup until smooth using an immersion blender.
6. Stir in most of the fresh basil, saving a few leaves for garnish, and serve hot.

## Notes

- For a creamier soup, stir in a splash of cream or coconut milk just before serving.
- Freezes well for up to 3 months in an airtight container.
